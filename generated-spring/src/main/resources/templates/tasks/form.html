About IPFS Storage

Your task data will be uploaded to IPFS (InterPlanetary File System), a decentralized storage network. A unique Content Identifier (CID) will be generated and stored in the database for retrieval.

Quick Tips
  • Tasks are automatically uploaded to IPFS when created or updated

  • All CIDs remain accessible on the IPFS network permanently
